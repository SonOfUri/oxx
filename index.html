The 0xX Bot

Our first utility 'The 0xX Bot' is evolved to handle multiple tweet categories like 'Elon tweets', 'AI News', 'Meme News', etc. It's a comprehensive tool to keep the community informed about diverse topics of interest. Simply share tweets in our dedicated Telegram group, under the relevant topic. The bot will automatically categorize and archive them in our UI, corresponding to the selected topic. Navigate through different categories of tweets, explore top contributors, and stay updated with ease. Our interactive interface makes it simple to follow the topics you care about and master X in a profitiable manner.

With an integrated 'launches' section see the first movers launched off of breaking tweets, complete with CA and a full audit. You'll never need another place to find great launches at such speed.
